About Us

About The KIET Group of Institutions, Ghaziabad

KIET was established in 1998 with the Intake of 198 students. The Institute has more than 5000 students on its rolls in 2017-18 : a substantial growth in 19 years to meet the need of quality technical education in U.P. We have pioneered technological, pharmaceutical and management education by offering Bachelor and Master programs in these fields. Most of the department are accredited by NBA. Also, KIET is accredited by NAAC with “A” Grade. KIET has been maintaining excellent results over the last several years and has been consistently rated as one of the best Institution of AKTU (formerly UPTU)

KIET School of Management was established in the year 2004 which offers Master in Business Administration (MBA) program. This two year full time course, affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow. The department is accredited by NBA. The training is imparted with specialization in Human Resource, Marketing Management, Finance, Information Technology and International Business.

About Southwestern Oklahoma State University USA (SWOSU):

Established in 1901, Southwestern Oklahoma State University has a long-standing reputation for high quality academic programs and friendly service to students, alumni, and friends. Students may complete associate, bachelor's, master's, and doctoral degrees while attending either the traditional on-campus classes or participating in distance learning opportunities. SWOSU offers over 70 undergraduate degrees and 20 graduate degrees so students have a variety of choices. Located in the center of western Oklahoma in the beautiful city of Weatherford, students enjoy the interaction of a thriving community that is only 45 minutes away from the Oklahoma City metro area. With an enrollment of over five thousand students, there are many opportunities to participate in student organizations and exciting athletic events. SWOSU is often described as a “small campus with a big campus feel.”

SWOSU’s Everett Dobson School of Business and Technology has earned recognition as one of the best business and technology schools in Oklahoma. With a current enrollment of 950, the student population is a rich blend of local and international students. Students have the opportunity to earn degrees in bachelor of business administration, computer science, or engineering technology. Graduate degrees are available as well. The business programs and engineering technology programs are fully accredited, with computer science pursuing accreditation in the near future. Graduates of the business, computer science, and technology programs are hired by various companies in Oklahoma, as well as across the United States and internationally.

About MTC Global:

MTC Global - a global think tank in higher education and an Apex Advisory Body in Management Education in the world having presence in over 30+ countries, over 1000 B-Schools, 30,000 + members, 45 national chapters, 32 international chapters and connected with millions of people across the world through its different initiatives. A participant in United National Global Compact program, United Nations Academic Impact and ISO 9001: 2008 certified organization.

MTC Global are partner organization with United Nations for UN Global Compact and UN Academic Impact in line with implementation of Millennium Developmental Goals.

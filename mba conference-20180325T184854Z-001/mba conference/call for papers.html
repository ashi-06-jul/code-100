Call For Papers

The conference will focus on high quality research and industrial papers on theory, design and implementation of the emerging science and technology, including all the interdisciplinary areas under the following major disciplines, but it is not limited to:

  • Analytical development of drugs and chemicals
  • Bio Mathematics
  • Biodegradable Polymers
  • Computational Mechanics
  • Current developments in Science and Technology
  • Distributed Computing
  • Environmental Friendly Materials
  • Fluid Mechanics
  • Graph theory
  • Green Technologies and sustainable environment
  • Image and signal Processing
  • Information and Coding Theory
  • Liquid Crystal and their Nano Composites
  • Mathematical Modelling
  • Nano Materials
  • Natural Products
  • Network Analysis
  • Nuclear and Particle Physics
  • Optimization Techniques
  • Pesticide residue analysis
  • Plasmonics
  • Soft computing Techniques
  • Waste management and energy production
  • Water treatment and reuse


Topics Covered

Research or Review Paper from all the Branch of Engineering, Science, Humanities and Management are welcome.
